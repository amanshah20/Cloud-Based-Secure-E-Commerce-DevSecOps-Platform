Our Story

Born from a passion for quality, built for those who refuse to settle.

Est. 2020

We believe in quality over everything

LuxeMarket was founded with one simple idea: people deserve premium products without the premium markup. We partner directly with artisans, manufacturers, and boutique brands to bring you the finest things in life.

Every product in our catalog is hand-selected by our curation team. If it doesn't meet our exacting standards, it doesn't make the cut. We're obsessed with quality so you don't have to be.

Based in Mumbai, we serve customers across India and ship to over 20 countries worldwide. Our commitment to exceptional service is what keeps our customers coming back.
